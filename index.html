Ube - Our Signature

Ube, a creamy, subtly sweet Philippine delicacy, is the heart of SHAKEZ’s iconic purple vibe. Our vegan, low-sugar milkshakes use natural sugars from fruits and ube jam—add sugar if you want it sweeter! We’re redefining the ube space with unforgettable flavors. Good Mood. Great Taste. SHAKEZ.
