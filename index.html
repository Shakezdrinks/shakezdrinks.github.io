I'm Mohamed Sheikh. As a kid, I was teased and called "Milkshake." That inspired SHAKEZ, a milkshake brand specializing in ube, a creamy Philippine delicacy. Like Blank Street redefined coffee, we’re making milkshakes a daily ritual—better than coffee, with no aftertaste. #ILoveShakez

Discover SHAKEZ

Ube - Our Signature

Ube, a creamy, subtly sweet Philippine delicacy, gives SHAKEZ its iconic purple twist. Every shake is ube-enhanced, but you can opt out for a custom vibe.

Our Menu

Build your perfect shake with our barista steps. Every flavor is ube-enhanced for a purple twist, designed for moods like focus, calm, or energy. Serve hot or cold, like a latte.

How to SHAKEZ

  1. Milk Base: Choose your lactose-free base.
  2. Flavors + Ube: Pick a mood-driven flavor, with or without ube.
  3. Size: Select MiniZ, ClassicZ, or MaxZ.
  4. Thickness Level: Opt for LowZ (coffee-like), MediumZ, or HighZ.
  5. Toppings: Add strawberry, chocolate, or more.
  6. Options: Choose hot/cold, caffeine, or adaptogens.

The final result: Your perfect SHAKEZ!
